Privacy Policy for CardStaq

Last updated: March 8, 2026

CardStaq helps users store card information locally on their device.

Data Collection

CardStaq does not collect personal data on our servers.

Camera Access

CardStaq may request access to the camera to scan barcodes. Camera data is used only for scanning and is not uploaded to our servers.

Local Storage

Card data is stored locally on the user’s device.

In-App Purchases

CardStaq offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited cards. Purchases are processed by Apple.
